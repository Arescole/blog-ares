On the first day of my internship, I focused on observing the office environment and understanding workplace dynamics. Day 4: Internship/OJT Journey

On the fourth day of my internship, I shifted my focus towards document management by starting the process of segregating printed resolutions for the entire province of Surigao del Sur. This task required a methodical approach, as I had to carefully sort through stacks of resolutions, ensuring that each document was placed in the correct category. The sheer volume of paperwork was daunting, but I steadily worked my way through it. I found this experience insightful, as it gave me a deeper understanding of the legislative process and how resolutions are organized for reference. The task also honed my organizational skills, as I had to develop an efficient system to categorize the resolutions for easier access later on. By the end of the day, I had made significant progress, setting the groundwork for the next few days of document sorting.
